Project 3

Design & simulation of Sterling Engine in SolidWorks

A sterling engine is a heat engine that converts heat into mechanical work. It's kind of like a steam engine, but instead of using steam, it uses a gas like air or helium. The gas is heated and cooled in a cycle, which makes it expand and contract. This pushes on a piston, which turns a crankshaft and creates motion.
